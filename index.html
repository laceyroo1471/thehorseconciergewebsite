The Platform

The whole picture,
in one trusted place.

Horse ownership is a beautifully complex. Farrier cycles, vet records, supplement logs, trainer notes — scattered across old texts, email threads, and memory. The Horse Concierge brings every thread together into a single, elegant record that moves with your horse, wherever they go.

Built by a third-generation horsewoman who understood that the people who care for your horse deserve the same level of information you carry in your head.

01
Complete Care Records

Every vet visit, ride, farrier cycle, feed change, and injury note — logged in one digital record that travels with your horse for life.

02
Trusted Provider Directory

4,000+ verified farriers, vets, trainers, and specialists with ratings and reviews. Searchable by location. No more word-of-mouth scrambles when you need someone fast.

03
Team Sharing

Invite your entire care team. Your vet, farrier, and trainer see exactly what you see — before they ever see the horse.

04
Pattern Recognition

Spot patterns across health and performance over time. Proactive care starts with a full picture, not scattered memory.
